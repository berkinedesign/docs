## TikTok Configuration

Follow these steps to setup your TikTok Developer Account and include your API keys in Davinci AI. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to <a href="https://developers.tiktok.com/" target="_blank">TikTok Developer Portal</a> and sign in there, afterwards click on **Developer Portal**.
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-1.png" />       
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Select **Manage Apps** and click on **Connect an App**. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-2.png" /> 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Provide App Name and click on **Confirm** button. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-3.png" /> 
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    On the next page, under the **Basic information** section, fill in the required fields as you prefer. Below is an example of how it was filled for our demo. 
    <Note>TikTok requires you to verify your domain URL with DNS TXT record, some helpful guidance can be found <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ggwnt_9NDoc" target="_blank">here</a></Note> 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-4.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 5">
    Click on **Add products** button. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-6.png" />
    And add following products as shown below. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-5.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
    After adding your products, you will see a new **Login Kit** section appear, enter your TikTok callback URL, which is **https://YOUR_DOMAIN_NAME/app/oauth/callback/facebook**  
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-7.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 7">
    Under **Content Posting API** section, make sure you have enabled the checkbox for **Direct Post** and have **verified** your domain.    
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-8.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 8">
    On the **Add Scopes** section, make sure to add all scopes. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-9.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 9">
    Now go to **Credentials** section and copy your **Client Key** and **Cleint Secretn** credentials.
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-10.png" />
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 10">
    <Warning>After you are done with filling all fields, make sure to click on **Submit for review** button. Tiktok and need to proper business identification</Warning>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 11">
    1. Go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel, click on **Extensions** tab, and select **Social Media Suite** extension. 
    2. Under **TikTok Settings** section, include your **TikTok Client Key** and **TikTok Client Secret** with the Client Key and Client Secret values that you just copied earlier. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/tiktok-11.png" /> 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 12">
    After you have included all API keys in the **TikTok Settings** section, click **Save** button. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 13">
    You know have completed the TikTok configuration. 
  </Step>
</Steps>
