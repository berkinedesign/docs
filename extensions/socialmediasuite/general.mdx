## Social Media Suite Settings

Please follow the steps below on how to configure Social Media Suite extension. <br />

<Note>Make sure that you are running v7.2 of Davinci AI, this is the minimum required version for the latest Social Media Suite extension.</Note>


<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    First make sure you have your domain URL set under **Admin Panel** -> **General Settings** -> **Global Settings**.  <br />
    Set your **Website URL** to be matching your domain name. Make sure that it does **not** end it "/" at the end of your domain name. It is very important to receive proper callbacks from Social Media providers later.  
    <Note>Please note that you cannot run Social Media Suite extension on localhost as Social Networks do not accept localhost URL as callback address.</Note>
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/general-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **AI Social Media Suite** extension after successfully purchasing it first.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/general-2.png" /> 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Next, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel, click on **Extensions** tab, and select **Social Media Suite** extension. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/social-media/general-3.png" /> 
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    Turn on the checkboxes for **AI Social Media Feature** and **AI Social Media Free Tier Access** 
    <Note>If you don't turn on AI Social Media Free Tier Access, then users who did not subscribe to any subscription plan where you have AI Social Media feature enabled, will not have any access to this feature</Note>
    <Warning>Subscription plans are only available for **Extended License** users</Warning>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 5">
    Now configure each of your Social Media accounts and include their API keys into respective sections below. <br />
    For your convenience we have included step by step guide for each and every Social Media platforms below. <br />
    You have full control on which Social Media platform to enable for your customers.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
    After you have included all API keys as needed click **Save** button. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7">
    You know have completed the AI Social Media Suite extension. 
  </Step>
</Steps>
