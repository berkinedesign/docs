## Extension Configuration
To be able to use Mailchimp feature, follow these steps to setup Mailchimp Extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **Mailchimp** extension.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Extensions** tab there. Click on **Mailchimp** there. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Register with Mailchimp:
    1. Go to <a href="https://mailchimp.com/" target="_blank">Mailchimp</a> website and sign up with them in case if don't have an account with them.
    2. After your are logged into your Mailchimp account, click on your user at the top right corder and select **Account & billing** option. 
        <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-4.png" />
    3. On your Account page, click on **API keys** under **Extras tab** 
        <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-5.png" />
    4. Generate your API key and copy it.  
        <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-6.png" />
    5. Now on the left menu panel, click on **Audience** and select **Settings** tab.  
        <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-7.png" />
    6. Copy your **Audience ID** value.  
        <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-8.png" />
    <Note>Store your API key securely and never share it publicly. The key can provide direct access to your account.</Note>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    To control the settings of **Mailchimp** exension, return to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on the **Extensions** tab, and select **Mailchimp** extension.
    Configuration of Mailchimp is done on this page, there will be no new menu item added in the Admin Panel. Below is the explanation of each field on Mailchimp page. 
    - Enter your **Mailchimp API Key**
    - Enter your **Mailchimp List ID**
    - Turn on **Add Free Tier User Group** if you want to Synchronize non-subscribers accounts only with your Mailchimp account
    - Turn on **Add Subscriber User Group** if you want to Synchronize subscribers accounts only with your Mailchimp account
    <img src="/images/extensions/mailchimp/mail-3.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You know have completed the Mailchimp extension configuration.  
  </Step>
</Steps>
