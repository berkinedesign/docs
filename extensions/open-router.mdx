## Extension Configuration
To be able to use OpenRouter feature, follow these steps to setup OpenRouter Extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **OpenRouter** extension.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/openrouter/open-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Extensions** tab there. Click on **OpenRouter** there. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/openrouter/open-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Register with OpenRouter:
    1. Go to <a href="https://openrouter.ai/" target="_blank">OpenRouter</a> website and sign up with them in case if don't have an account with them.
    2. After your are logged into your OpenRouter account, click on the **API Keys** and click on **Create Key** button. 
        <img src="/images/extensions/openrouter/open-3.png" />
    3. Also make sure you fill up your credits under **Credits** tab in your OpenRouter account as OpenRouter does not offer any free credits to use their platform. 
        <Note>Store your API key securely and never share it publicly. The key can provide direct access to your account.</Note>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    To control the settings of **OpenRouter** exension, return to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on the **Extensions** tab, and select **OpenRouter** extension.
    Configuration of OpenRouter is done on this page, there will be no new menu item added in the Admin Panel. Below is the explanation of each field on OpenRouter page. 
    - Enter your **OpenRouter API Key**
    - Turn on **Use OpenRouter** checkbox if you want to use your OpenRouter account instead other vendors, and AI Writer and AI Chat features will automatically start using OpenRouter for supported models. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/openrouter/open-4.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You know have completed the OpenRouter extension configuration.  
  </Step>
</Steps>
