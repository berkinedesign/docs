## Extension Configuration
To be able to use Hubspot feature, follow these steps to setup Hubspot Extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Go to the **Marketplace** in **Admin Panel** and install **Hubspot** extension.     
    <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-1.png" />    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    After installation is completed, go to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on **Extensions** tab there. Click on **Hubspot** there. 
    <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Register with HubSpot:
    1. Go to <a href="https://hubspot.com" target="_blank">HubSpot</a> website and sign up with them in case if don't have an account with them.
    2. After your logged into your HubSpot account, click on the **Settings** button at the top right corner
        <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-3.png" />
    3. On the left menu panel, go to **Integrations** - **Private Apps** page and click on **Create a private app** button. 
        <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-4.png" />
    4. Enter app name along with some description 
        <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-5.png" />
    5. On the scopes section, include all scopes for **CRM** and **CSM** scopes.  
        <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-6.png" />
    6. Last section on webhooks there is optional, then click on **Create App** button.  
    7. After you have created your app, you will be shown **Access Token**, copy it.    
        <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-7.png" />
        <Note>Store your Access Token securely and never share it publicly. The key can provide direct access to your account.</Note>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Step 4">
    To control the settings of **HubSpot** exension, return to **AI Settings** page in Admin Panel and click on the **Extensions** tab, and select **HubSpot** extension.
    Configuration of Hubspot is done on this page, there will be no new menu item added in the Admin Panel. Below is the explanation of each field on Hubspot page. 
    - Enter your **Hubspot Access Token**
    - Turn on **Add Free Tier User Group** if you want to Synchronize non-subscribers accounts only with your HubSpot account
    - Turn on **Add Subscriber User Group** if you want to Synchronize subscribers accounts only with your HubSpot account
    - Afterwards click on **Save & Add Users** button
    <Note>Note that after you properly configure your HubSpot account with valid Access Token, all new users that are yet to register will get automatically added to your HubSpot account.</Note>
    <img src="/images/extensions/hubspot/hubspot-8.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    You know have completed the Hubspot extension configuration.  
  </Step>
</Steps>
